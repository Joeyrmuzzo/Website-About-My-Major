Duties of a security specialist

The duties of a security specialist include but are not limited to, designing and implementing saftey measures and controls, monitoring network activity to identify vulnerable points, and acting on privacy breaches and malware threats.

The expected growth for security specialist jobs is high. There is a large demand for security especially on mobile devices. There have been many major security breaches that have affected companies and their users. It has generated lots of media attention. There has been a public outcry for better security reguarding personal data. This has created a demand for security specialists.

The average salary for a security specialist ranges from $40,000 - $100,000. This is due to the range in experience and skill. This job requires lots of experience which accounts for the range in salary. The more work experience a person has the higher they can expect their salary to be.

Typically working conditions for a security specialist can vary. They might work out of an office building or from home. They can also work as a free lance contractor and might be required to tavel all over the country.
