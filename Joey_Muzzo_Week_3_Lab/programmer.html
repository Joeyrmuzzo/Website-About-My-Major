Duties of a programmer

The duties of a programmer include but are not limited to, write programs in a variety of languages, update and expand existing programs, debug programs, test programs, and build and use computer-assisted software engineering tools.

The expected growth for programmer positions is good. The world is becomming more technologically oriented so there is a greater demand for programmers. The job market has seen a rise in the availability of programmer jobs.

The expected salary for a programmer can range anywhere between $60,000 - $80,000. Depending on the experience level and skills aquired the salary can vary.

The typical working conditions for a programmer are similar to office working conditions. Programmers usually work from a office or possible from home. The minimum requirements are a computer, desk, and chair. That is all I need to work on a program.
